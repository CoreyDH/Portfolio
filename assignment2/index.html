About Me

Corey is an aspiring web developer that has been in the web design and support fields professionally since 2012.


His humble beginnings started all the way back in 1998 when he was a part of websites such as Galaxy Games Network and Diablo 2 Frequency. While not widely known, it did spur his interests in web development as a possible career choice. However due to unclear goals at the time, it would not come until much later in life that he realized his true passion was there all along.


Striving to get into his renewed career interest as soon as possible, he joined a coding bootcamp hoping to accelerate his learning and some day land that dream job. His story is not yet finished, and one day that dream will be realized.
